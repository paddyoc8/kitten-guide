üìã Room Expansion Protocol

Follow these steps to gradually expand your kitten's territory

1 Extend Safe Room Time

Increase your time in the safe room to 30-45 minutes, reading or working quietly. Begin initiating play sessions and extend them to 15-20 minutes twice daily.

2 Begin Handling Training

Start gentle handling and recall training during visits. Practice touching paws, ears, mouth. Begin carrier training by leaving it open with treats inside.

3 First Room Expansion

Open adjacent room while monitoring but not following. Leave safe room door open as retreat. Avoid play until kitten shows confidence in new space.

Duration: Start with 15-30 minutes, guide back to safe room. Gradually increase time as confidence grows.

4 Add Resources to New Space

Place water bowl and second litter box in expanded area. Transfer some old litter to new box to encourage use.

5 Gradual Expansion

Add one new room every few days. Always maintain safe room access and continue overnight containment for several more weeks.
