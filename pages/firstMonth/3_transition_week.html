🏠 Transition Week

Guide your kitten's gradual expansion from safe room to full home access

✅ Signs of Readiness for Expansion

Look for these positive indicators before opening up new spaces:

😸 Confident Behavior

  • Exploring freely in safe room
  • Playing with toys regularly
  • Approaching you readily
  • Showing curiosity about beyond the door

🚽 Reliable Habits

  • Consistently using litter box
  • No accidents outside box
  • Normal eating and drinking
  • Regular appetite patterns

😌 Calm Body Language

  • Relaxed posture when resting
  • Normal grooming behavior
  • Purring during interactions
  • Displaying playful behavior

📋 Room Expansion Protocol

Follow these steps to gradually expand your kitten's territory

1 Extend Safe Room Time

Increase your time in the safe room to 30-45 minutes, reading or working quietly. Begin initiating play sessions and extend them to 15-20 minutes twice daily.

2 Begin Handling Training

Start gentle handling and recall training during visits. Practice touching paws, ears, mouth. Begin carrier training by leaving it open with treats inside.

3 First Room Expansion

Open adjacent room while monitoring but not following. Leave safe room door open as retreat. Avoid play until kitten shows confidence in new space.

Duration: Start with 15-30 minutes, guide back to safe room. Gradually increase time as confidence grows.

4 Add Resources to New Space

Place water bowl and second litter box in expanded area. Transfer some old litter to new box to encourage use.

5 Gradual Expansion

Add one new room every few days. Always maintain safe room access and continue overnight containment for several more weeks.

⚠️
Scale back if you see: Hiding immediately in new spaces, refusing to eat, having accidents, or showing anxiety. Give more time in safe room before trying again.

📊 Track Your Progress

🗓️ Day-by-Day Tracking

  • Day 1: Extended safe room time
  • Day 2-3: Handling training begun
  • Day 4-5: First room opened
  • Day 6-7: Resources added to new room
  • Week 2: Additional rooms opened

🎯 Success Indicators

  • ✅ Kitten explores new room within 10 minutes
  • ✅ Uses new litter box without hesitation
  • ✅ Returns to safe room voluntarily
  • ✅ Maintains normal eating schedule
  • ✅ Shows playful behavior in new spaces
  • ✅ Sleeps peacefully in safe room at night
💡
Pro Tip: Let your kitten set the pace. Some may be ready for expansion in 3-4 days, while others need 1-2 weeks. Watch their body language and behavior rather than following a strict timeline.
