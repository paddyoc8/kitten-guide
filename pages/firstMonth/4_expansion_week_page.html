🌟 Expansion Week

Gradually expanding your kitten's territory and social circle

👥 Socialization

Begin socialising kitten with new people in safe room in same manner as the initiation period.

🏠 House Access

Begin unsupervised downstairs access for 30-60 minutes while you're home.

🏺 Permanent Resources

Add permanent resources to expanded area: second litter box in convenient (ideally permanent) location, additional water station (also in new permanent location) away from food area.

🪜 Stairs Exploration

Keep upstairs doors closed in case kitten is ready to explore stairs. Once kitten has tackled stairs, begin upstairs room explorations in same manner as transition period.

🌙 Overnight Containment

Continue overnight containment in safe room for several more weeks, even with daytime house access.

🧳 Carrier Training

While in the safe room, progress carrier training to shutting the door and lifting the carrier.
