🏠 First Few Days at Home

Building trust and routine during the critical adjustment period

🕐
Remember: The first few days are crucial for building trust and establishing routines. Every kitten adjusts at their own pace—follow their lead and remain patient.

📅 First Week Timeline

A gentle approach to helping your kitten settle in

Day 1

Arrival & Safe Room

Let kitten explore safe room at their own pace. Minimal interaction.

Days 2-3

Building Trust

Short, gentle visits. Let kitten approach you. Begin basic handling.

Days 4-5

Extending Comfort

Longer visits if kitten is comfortable. Gentle play sessions.

Days 6-7

Gradual Expansion

Consider expanding territory if confident. Maintain routines.

🎮 Play Session Guidelines

Proper play is essential for bonding and healthy development

Duration: Keep sessions short and gentle (5-10 minutes)
Initiation: Let the kitten initiate play when possible
Toys: Use soft toys, string toys, or wands
Ending: Slow toy movement to mimic it 'becoming tired'
Reward: End with a treat and put toy away
Safety: Stop if kitten pants or becomes frantic

🎯 Signs Kitten Wants to Play

  • 🐾 Pouncing on fingers or shoelaces
  • 🏐 Batting at hanging objects
  • 🎪 Play bows (front end down, rear up)
  • 🧸 Bringing toys to you
  • ⚡ Sudden bursts of energy
  • 🍃 Chasing their tail or shadows

✅ Extend Visits When You See

  • 😊 Kitten approaches confidently
  • 🎵 Purring when you're present
  • 😌 Relaxed body language
  • 👀 Curious exploration behavior
  • 🤲 Seeking pets or attention
  • 🛏️ Comfortable enough to nap nearby

🎯 Key Principle

Follow their lead rather than a strict timeline. Every kitten adjusts differently!

🤲 Socialization & Handling

Begin gentle handling to help your kitten become comfortable with human touch

📋 Handling Checklist

  • Begin with very short handling periods (30 seconds)
  • Gently touch ears to get kitten used to ear cleaning
  • Handle paws for future nail trimming
  • Stroke chin and face for bonding
  • Use treats during handling sessions
  • Offer gentle praise throughout
  • Gradually increase handling time as kitten relaxes

🔊 Sound Management

  • 🔇 Keep household sounds at low volume
  • 📺 Introduce TV/music gradually
  • 🚪 Minimize door slamming
  • 📱 Keep phone volumes low
  • 🥄 Be gentle with dishes and utensils
  • 👥 Brief guests on quiet behavior
⚠️
Avoid Overwhelming: Never force interactions. If your kitten shows signs of stress (hiding, hissing, trembling), give them more time and space. Some kittens need several days to a week to feel comfortable.

📝 Daily Observations

Track your kitten's adjustment progress

✅ Positive Signs

  • 🍽️ Eating and drinking regularly
  • 🚽 Using litter box consistently
  • 😴 Sleeping peacefully
  • 🎮 Showing interest in play
  • 👋 Approaching humans
  • 🔍 Curious exploration
  • 🎵 Purring or vocalizing

🚨 Watch For Concerns

  • 🚫 Not eating for 24+ hours
  • 💧 Not drinking water
  • 🚽 Avoiding litter box
  • 😰 Excessive hiding (48+ hours)
  • 🤢 Vomiting or diarrhea
  • 😵 Extreme lethargy
  • 🌡️ Signs of illness
🎉
Success Tip: Every small interaction is progress! Celebrate when your kitten takes treats from your hand, purrs in your presence, or shows curiosity about their new home. Building trust takes time, but the reward of a confident, loving companion is worth the patience.
