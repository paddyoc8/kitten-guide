🌟 Signs You're Doing Great

Positive indicators that you and your kitten are thriving together

🎉 You're Being an Amazing Cat Parent!

These positive signs show that you're providing excellent care for your kitten

😸 Social Comfort

  • Your kitten approaches you readily and doesn't hide when you enter

🍽️ Healthy Habits

  • They're eating, drinking, and using litter box normally

🧠 Understanding Behavior

  • You can tell the difference between normal and concerning behaviors

🎮 Enjoyable Play

  • Play sessions are enjoyable for both of you

🤲 Confident Care

  • You feel comfortable handling your kitten for basic care

⚕️ Good Judgment

  • You know when to worry vs. when to wait and observe

💕 Strong Bond

  • Your kitten shows signs of bonding (purring, following, slow blinking)
🎯
Keep Up the Great Work! These positive signs indicate a healthy, happy relationship between you and your kitten. Trust your instincts and continue providing the loving care you're already giving.
