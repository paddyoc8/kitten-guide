❓ Questions Before Adopting

Essential questions and observations to make the best choice for your family

0% Complete

🗣️ Questions for the Foster/Breeder

Ask these important questions to understand your kitten's background and needs:

1
When was the kitten born?
Important for knowing developmental stage and vaccination timing
2
Was the kitten raised with its mother?
Affects socialisation and behavior development
3
Is the kitten friendly?
Helps gauge temperament and socialisation level
4
Is the kitten healthy?
Ask about any medical issues or treatments
5
Where did it spend 1-9 weeks old?
Critical socialisation period location impacts behavior

👀 Things to Observe During Your Visit

Look for these important signs during your visit:

👶
Can you see the kitten with its mother? Observe their interaction and bonding
😸
Is the mother healthy and friendly? This indicates genetics and quality of early care
🏠
Are there many other cats/litters? High numbers can indicate poor care or puppy mill conditions
✨
Is the environment clean? Look for odours, cleanliness, and organisation
💪
Does the kitten look healthy? Clear eyes, clean ears, active behaviour
❤️
By visit end: was kitten friendly, nervous, or fearful? Note their overall temperament

⚠️ Essential Considerations

Important topics to discuss and arrange:

🔍
Microchipping

Permanent identification for safety - ensure this is done or arrange it

🪱
Worming

When was it last done and what treatment was used?

💉
Vaccinating

What's been done and what vaccinations are still needed?

✂️
Neutering

Timing discussion and clarify if it's your responsibility or theirs

🏥
Pet Insurance

Research options and arrange coverage before pickup day

💡
Confidence Tip: A reputable breeder or foster will welcome these questions and provide detailed answers. If they seem evasive or annoyed, this is a red flag. Good caregivers want to ensure their kittens go to prepared, caring homes.
📝
Pro Tip: Write down the answers during your visit or bring this list with you. It's easy to forget important details when you're excited about meeting your potential new companion!
