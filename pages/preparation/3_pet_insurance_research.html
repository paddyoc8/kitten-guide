Standard Exclusions: Pet insurance usually does not cover the following items. Make sure you understand what's excluded from your policy.

Routine care

Check-ups, nail trimming, grooming - Regular maintenance and wellness visits are typically not covered.

Preventive care

Vaccinations, flea/tick/worm treatment - Standard preventive treatments are usually excluded.

Neutering/spaying

Elective surgical procedures are typically not covered.

Dental cleaning

Routine dental hygiene is usually excluded (vs. disease treatment).

Pre-existing conditions

Conditions present before policy start date are typically excluded (unless disclosed and later accepted).
