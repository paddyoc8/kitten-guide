🔮 Future Planning

Use the following questions to guide your decisions and set your kitten up for a comfortable, happy life in your home.

📊 Planning Progress

0%

Complete all planning considerations to ensure you're ready for your kitten's future!

💤 Where Will My Cat Sleep Long-Term?

Do I want my cat to sleep in my bedroom?
What are the quiet and cosy spots in the home that would be best for cat beds?
💡 Tip: Cats love warmth, elevation, and enclosed spaces. Try placing a bed near a window or in a quiet corner with a view.

🍽️ Where Will My Cat Eat Long-Term?

Where will be the best permanent feeding spot?
Where could a water bowl go if cats prefer to have their water bowl separate?
💡 Tip: Avoid placing food near loud appliances.

🧴 Where Will the Litter Box Go Long-Term?

Is the litter box in a quiet, private location where my cat feels safe?
Will I be able to clean this spot easily and frequently?
💡 Tip: Make sure it's not in a place that gets too hot, cold, or noisy (like next to a washing machine).

🏠 How Will My Cat's Territory Expand Over Time?

Will there be any rooms off-limits to the cat?
Are there spaces I need to "cat-proof" (cords, plants, breakables) before allowing access?
Do I want to provide vertical spaces like shelves, cat trees, or window perches in different rooms?
Will we be planning to add in a cat flap for when the cat is ready to go outside?
💡 Tip: Cats think vertically. Giving them access to different levels of a room helps them feel secure and enriched.

📅 Routine Planning

🍽️ Feeding Schedule

Who will feed the cat, and when? How will we track when the cat has been fed?

🧹 Litter Box Maintenance

Who's responsible when? Where will any litter related products be stored?

🚫 Boundaries

What do we want to be our household rules for the cat?

🤝 How Will This Affect Our Routine Together?

Am I okay with a cat on the sofa, dining table, or counters—or do I need to start setting boundaries now?
Will this routine still work if our lifestyle changes (e.g., new job, travel, new pets)?

🧠 Other Things to Consider

Future proofing: As your kitten grows into an adult, are your plans flexible enough to adapt to their preferences and needs?
Health and mobility: Will your chosen litterbox location or sleeping spot be accessible if your cat ever develops joint issues or disabilities?
Travel and boarding: Do you have a plan for where your cat will stay during vacations or emergencies?
