🧸 Initial Toy Supplies

Build up toys gradually and regularly rotate the toys available for the cat

📦 Toy Storage & Organization

🎾 Solo Toys

Toys your kitten can play with independently

  • Lightweight balls
  • Fabric Mice
  • Puzzle Toys

🪶 Interactive Toys

Toys that require human interaction for playtime

  • Feather wands
  • String Toys

👂 Sensory Toys

Toys that stimulate different senses

  • Toilet rolls
  • Crinkle balls
  • Rustling paper bag (handles removed)
  • Soft carrying toys
💡
Toy Rotation Tip: Keep some toys stored away and rotate them weekly to maintain your kitten's interest. This makes "old" toys feel new and exciting again!
